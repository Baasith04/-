Mini Regulated Power Supply

A Mini Regulated Power Supply is a compact circuit that provides a stable and consistent voltage output. It ensures smooth power delivery by regulating fluctuations from the input source. This system typically includes components like voltage regulators, capacitors, and diodes for efficiency. It is widely used in electronics projects, embedded systems, and testing applications.
